# ClinicFlow V1 - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0
Status: Planning
Author: Dev Bhardwaj

---

# 1. Introduction

ClinicFlow is an AI-powered appointment booking system designed for small and medium clinics.

The goal is to help clinics digitize patient appointments without requiring expensive software or additional staff.

Patients should be able to visit the clinic's website, learn about the clinic and its doctors, and book appointments through WhatsApp. The AI receptionist will collect the necessary information and automatically store appointments in Google Sheets.

This MVP is focused on being simple, affordable, and easy for clinics to adopt.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many clinics still manage appointments through phone calls, WhatsApp messages, handwritten registers, or manual spreadsheets.

This creates several problems:

- Staff repeatedly answer the same questions.
- Appointment details get lost in chats.
- Doctors' schedules are difficult to manage.
- Patients often forget appointments.
- Small clinics cannot afford expensive management software.

ClinicFlow solves these problems using an AI receptionist and simple automation.

---

# 3. Goals

The primary goals of ClinicFlow V1 are:

- Build a professional clinic website.
- Allow patients to book appointments through WhatsApp.
- Automatically record appointments in Google Sheets.
- Reduce receptionist workload.
- Keep the system simple enough for any clinic to use.

Included in Version 1

- Modern clinic website
- Doctor profiles
- Services page
- Contact page
- FAQ section
- WhatsApp appointment booking
- AI receptionist
- Google Sheets integration
- Google Calendar integration (optional)
- n8n automation

Not Included

- Admin Dashboard
- Login System
- Online Payments
- Billing
- Patient Portal
- Multi-clinic support
- Analytics
- SMS integration

---

# 6. Success Criteria

The MVP will be considered successful if:

- A patient can successfully book an appointment through WhatsApp.
- Appointment details are automatically saved.
- The clinic can easily view appointments.
- The AI correctly answers common clinic questions.
- The system can be demonstrated to potential clients.

## Emergency Cases

ClinicFlow V1 does not perform medical triage or determine whether a patient's condition is an emergency.

If a patient requests an appointment, the AI will continue with the normal booking process.

Medical advice and emergency handling are outside the scope of Version 1.
